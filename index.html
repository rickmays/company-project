Wild Life

Hay

Strutty's provides top quality coastal and alfalfa hay. Our coastal is produced in central Texas and our alfalfa comes to us from New Mexico. We carry coastal square bales and round bales and alfalfa in square bales.

We strive to provide the best and have our hay analyzed on a consistent basis and we are happy to provide customers with that analysis.
